# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

KlikVerder places high importance on the security of its systems and data. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it in a **responsible and confidential manner**.

**Contact:**  
security@klikverder.be

---

## Required Information

To ensure efficient assessment, please include:

- A clear and technical description of the vulnerability  
- Steps to reproduce the issue  
- The potential impact  
- Supporting evidence (proof-of-concept, screenshots, logs)  

---

## Handling of Reports

Upon receiving a report, KlikVerder will:

- Acknowledge receipt within **48 hours**  
- Assess the validity and impact  
- Provide periodic updates  
- Take appropriate remediation measures  

KlikVerder reserves the right to determine remediation priority and timelines based on severity and business impact.

---

## Responsible Disclosure

- Public disclosure is **not permitted** until the issue is resolved  
- A reasonable remediation timeframe must be respected  
- Recognition may be granted upon mutual agreement after resolution  

---

## Rewards

KlikVerder may, at its sole discretion, provide a **reward or recognition** for valid and impactful reports.

- Rewards are based on severity, impact, and report quality  
- No rights or entitlements can be derived from this  

---

## Scope

This policy applies only to systems and services owned or operated by KlikVerder.

Out of scope:

- Third-party systems  
- Social engineering  
- Physical security issues  

Unless there is a direct and demonstrable link to KlikVerder systems.

---

## Legal Safe Harbor

KlikVerder will not pursue legal action against individuals who:

- Act in good faith  
- Do not exploit the vulnerability  
- Do not access, modify, or delete data beyond what is necessary  
- Use findings strictly for research purposes  
- Report in accordance with this policy
